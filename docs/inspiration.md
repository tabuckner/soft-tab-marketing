## Visual Direction Decision — LOCKED

> **Status**: Approved 2026-02-11
> **Reference Picks**: C7, C8, D10, B11, M2, M7, M8

### Selected References & What We Take From Each

| Pick | What We Like |
|------|-------------|
| **C8** (IT Consultant) | Bold "Ship Your MVP" headline, stats row, bento-grid layout, device mockups, trust logos, modern dev-shop energy |
| **C7** (IT Consultancy) | Blue-dominant professional feel, real photography, comprehensive section structure, stats callouts |
| **B11** (Blue Landing Page) | **Dark mode north star.** Deep navy, massive centered headline with gradient text, ultra-clean, maximum confidence, subtle glow |
| **M8** (Minimal Corporate) | **Light mode north star.** Premium/sophisticated, large confident headings, architectural photography, stats with large numbers, generous whitespace, strong typography hierarchy |
| **M7** (Minimal Corporate) | Complete page structure reference — hero with photo overlay, icon service cards, case studies, FAQ accordion, contact form, well-organized footer |
| **M2** (Engineering) | Clean minimal feel, large hero image, muted palette with strategic color accents, lots of breathing room |
| **D10** (Web Agency Dark) | Dark section structure, process steps layout, services card grid on dark backgrounds |

### Visual Direction Summary

**Light Mode (Primary):** M8 meets C8
- Clean white backgrounds with generous whitespace
- Bold, large hero headline (Inter semibold, ~56-72px)
- Stats row below the fold for trust/credibility
- Service cards in structured grids with subtle icons
- Real photography where used (professional, not stock-feeling)
- Blue accent for CTAs and interactive elements only — not as background wash

**Dark Mode:** B11 as the north star
- Deep navy (`#0F1419`) base — not pure black
- Subtle gradient text on hero headline (blue→cyan brand gradient)
- Spacious, minimal, confident
- Cards/borders use `rgba(255,255,255,0.1)` — subtle, not heavy

### Recurring Patterns to Implement
- **Bold hero headlines** — large, confident typography, not decorative
- **Stats/trust rows** — number callouts (e.g. "50+ clients", "98% on-time") as social proof
- **Real photography** — business/architectural, not illustrations or 3D
- **Structured grid sections** — service cards in clean rows, bento-grid where appropriate
- **Generous whitespace** — content breathes, nothing cramped
- **Partner/client logos** — logo strip for credibility
- **Strategic blue accents** — CTAs and highlights only

### Visual "Don'ts"
- No busy/cluttered layouts — no competing visual elements
- No heavy gradient backgrounds or floating blobs
- No illustration-heavy aesthetics
- No rounded "friendly SaaS" style (bubble buttons, playful colors)
- No neon/glow effects
- No isometric devices, circuit boards, or AI-generated imagery
- No stock photography that feels generic
- No more than 2 font weights visible in any single section
